Surface Current

Surface Current is a continuous, directed movement of sea water driven by the wind. The Coriolis effect creates Ekman transport that forms five major gyres and a circumpolar current bounded by continents.

Deep Circulation

Deep Ocean Circulation(Thermohaline circulation) is part of the large-scale ocean circulation that is driven by the difference on deep ocean temperature and salt concentration.
